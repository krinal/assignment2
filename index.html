A Little Light Aims to Make Big Impact

OCTOBER 15, 2013
Erin Barry, Power Pitch

Meet Lucie she's a compact, waterproof, very cool-looking solar-powered lantern on a big mission to provide affordable and portable light to those who play or live "off the grid." From recreational campers, to those in the aftermath of a natural disaster, to the Samburu tribe in a remote part of Africa.
